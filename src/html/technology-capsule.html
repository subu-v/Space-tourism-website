SPACE CAPSULE

A space capsule is an often-crewed spacecraft that used a blunt-body reentry capsule to reenter the Earth's atmosphere without wings. Our capsule is where you'll spend your time during the flight. It includes a space gym, cinema and plenty of other activities to keep you entertained.
